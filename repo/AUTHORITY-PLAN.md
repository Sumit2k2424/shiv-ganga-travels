# Shiv Ganga Travels — Authority-Building Plan (fastest path)

Your on-site E-E-A-T is already strong: full Organization + founder schema,
Dataset/statistics hub, review markup, 60+ topical pages, GEO infrastructure.
The bottleneck now is **off-page**. This plan is ordered by speed-to-impact.

---

## TIER 1 — Reviews (do this week; biggest single lever)

You sit at 38 Google reviews / 4.6. Competitors like chardhamtour show 7,500+.
This gap hurts both rankings and AI citation trust more than any code change.

**The system (now built for you):** the short link `shivgangatravels.com/review`
redirects straight to your Google write-a-review form.

Use it relentlessly:
1. **WhatsApp follow-up** — 2 days after every yatra ends, send: *"Namaste 🙏
   We hope your Char Dham Yatra was blessed. If we served you well, a one-line
   Google review means the world to a small family operator: shivgangatravels.com/review"*
2. Put the link + a QR code on the **vehicle**, the **invoice**, and a small
   **card** handed out at drop-off.
3. Ask the driver-guide to mention it on the last day — in person converts best.

**Target:** 10–15 new reviews/month. At that rate you pass 150 within a year,
and the velocity itself (steady fresh reviews) is what Google weighs, not just
the total. Reply to every review — replies are a ranking signal.

When the real count climbs, update `reviewCount`/`ratingValue` in
`src/app/layout.js` so the schema matches reality.

## TIER 2 — Brand mentions on high-trust platforms (weeks 1–8)

Ahrefs-type studies consistently show unlinked brand mentions on Reddit,
YouTube and Quora predict AI visibility better than backlinks. AI engines
(ChatGPT, Google AI, Perplexity) lean heavily on these sources.

- **Reddit:** participate genuinely in r/india, r/uttarakhand, r/IndiaTravel,
  r/Dehradun threads about Char Dham. Answer real questions with useful detail;
  mention the operator only where natural. No spam — Reddit punishes it hard.
- **Quora:** answer "best Char Dham operator from Haridwar/Delhi", "Char Dham
  registration 2026", "Kedarnath helicopter booking" questions thoroughly.
  Link to your guide pages where they genuinely help.
- **YouTube:** even 3–4 honest phone-shot videos (a real yatra day, the
  registration process, the Kedarnath trek) with "Shiv Ganga Travels" in title
  + description. Video is the format AI summarizers increasingly cite.

posts), JustDial, Sulekha, TripAdvisor, MakeMyTrip/Thrillophilia listings,
